Meet The Man Behind the Meat

Pit Master Mike born and raised in Central Massachusetts comes to the Leblanc BBQ with no Food and Beverage experience.He Began training in 2021 as our BBQ Pit Master with Kansas City’s Best Pit Master.

Mike competes in many cooking and BBQ competitions. He is the only chef to have competed in many competitions but had zero recognition since he is not a cook, but does love barbeque.

Mike is well known all over the country – whether at his award-winning restaurants, special events, BBQ competitions, cooking alongside Wolf Gang Puck, James Beard House Chef or preparing tasty delights to Presidents George W. Bush and Bill Clinton.

Pit Master Mike has been teasing our guests with his mouthwatering, crave-worth BBQ and signature delights at 8 satellite locations at Leblanc BBQ and looks forward to creating smokin’ hot BBQ at the new Leblanc BBQ year-round restaurant.
